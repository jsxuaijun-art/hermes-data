# Study Plan Document Template (Chinese Education)

A reusable document structure for creating study plans targeting Shanghai middle-school students, with python-docx formatting.

## Typical Document Sections

1. **Cover page** — student profile, current stats, target school
2. **Gap analysis** — current ranking → target ranking per subject
3. **Admission path analysis** — school admission routes (自招/名额分配到区/名额分配到校/平行志愿), score thresholds
4. **Phased plan** — Grade 7 (基础重建) → Grade 8 (关键转折) → Grade 9 (冲刺)
5. **Daily/weekly schedule** — time allocation table per subject
6. **Key strategies** — 名额分配到校 focus, mistake notebook system, sleep-first policy
7. **3-year roadmap** — expected ranking improvement per semester
8. **Resource list** — exam paper sources, recommended 教辅

## School-Specific Notes

- **交大二附中**: 交附闵行 feeder school. Strong 名额分配到校 allocation. Key advantage for 名额分配到校 pathway.
- **名额分配到校 strategy**: Focus on intra-school ranking (校内排名), not district-wide. School rank matters more than district rank.
